NemoNemo Beam Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NemoNemo Beam ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game. By using NemoNemo Beam, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Device identifiers and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • Game session duration and frequency
  • Anonymous usage analytics
  • App Store/App Gallery attribution data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the game
  • Improving game performance and user experience
  • Analyzing player behavior and preferences
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

NemoNemo Beam uses the following third-party services:

  • App Store / Google Play: Distribution and update services
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal data)
  • Crash Reporting: Error tracking for app stability

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Retention

We retain your game data and usage statistics for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Game progress is stored locally on your device and in our servers for backup purposes. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about the data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

NemoNemo Beam is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: woweverstudio@gmail.com
